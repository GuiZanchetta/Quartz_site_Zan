# Guilherme Zanchetta
is a Brazilian researcher and artist working on sound, heritage, movement, and technology.
Current work focuses on intangible heritage through emerging technologies. He is a Research Fellow at LUME Teatro and a collaborator with IPEM–Ghent University on the FAPESP–FWO project “Singing in Between the Cracks” (Cantando F(r)estas), which explores XR and AI-mediated "living archives."

 - Currently an artist-in-residence at the Robotics, Automation and Dance Lab (https://the-rad-lab.org/)
 - Developer at the voice-driven game O Segundo Dia (https://store.steampowered.com/app/3392390/O_segundo_dia/)
 - Invited Researcher at NICS (https://www.nics.unicamp.br/)
