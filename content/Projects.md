## 🎭 artistic projects & collaborations

**Um Dia na Amazônia** - An immersive VR experience based on scientific data from the AmazonFACE project. The audience walks through a simulated Amazon rainforest with spatialized sound (ambisonics/binaural) and a full 24-hour cycle. Funded by PNAB. *Just approved — development starting now.*

**Lume Ghent** — Remote/telematic performance collaboration with artists in Ghent, Belgium and Lume Teatro in Campinas. Working with Sonobus and Reaper for real-time audio. 

**RAD Lab** — Artistic residency at the Robotics, Automation, and Dance Lab (Amy Laviers). Research on the relationship between movement, emotion, and robotics applied to dance and performance.

**ERIS** — Computer Music group at NICS (still taking shape)

**O Segundo Dia** — Game in development. Exploring sound design and game mechanics for voice interaction.

**Polifônio** — A interactive children book

**CCL** — Ongoing collaboration/research with CCL..
